How the sentiment score is calculated

For every day that has a prior day, four components are computed (each from −1 to +1) and blended into a score from −100 (max bearish) to +100 (max bullish):

  • Gap direction & size (30%) — opening gap vs the prior close; a 2% gap counts as full strength.
  • Candle body (25%) — green (close > open) is bullish, red is bearish, scaled by body size within the day's range.
  • Close location (25%) — closing near the high is bullish, near the low is bearish.
  • Gap integrity (20%) — a gap up that holds (doesn't trade back to prior close) is bullish; one that fills shows weakness. A gap down that fills/recovers is bullish.
